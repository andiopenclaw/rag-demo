What "grounded" means: the LLM only sees the retrieved chunks — it can't hallucinate beyond them
Chunking splits text into overlapping windows
Embeddings map meaning → vector space (all-MiniLM-L6-v2)
ChromaDB stores & searches vectors on disk
LLM generates answer from retrieved context (GPT-4o-mini)
